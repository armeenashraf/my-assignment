Lasagna Recipe


     Ingredients:

For Meat Sauce

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb bulk Italian sausage
  • 4 (15 oz) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 (16 oz) can sliced mushrooms, drained (optional)
  • 1 tsp garlic salt
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp dried thyme
  • ¼ tsp dried basil
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

For the cheese filling

  • 1 (15 oz) container ricotta cheese
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • ⅓ cup grated Parmesan cheese
